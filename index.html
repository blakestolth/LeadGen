How We Structure Your Loan

We don't just guess. We follow a strict logic path based on your Equity and Credit to find the vehicle that fits the bank's requirements.

Negative Equity

Loan Balance > Vehicle Value
(You owe more than it's worth)

Good Credit (700+)
Strategy: "Book It Out"

We find a specific vehicle (usually Truck/SUV) that banks value highly. We roll your negative equity into the new loan.
Result: Higher Payment, but clean exit.

New/Bad Credit (<650)
Strategy: The Cap

Banks limit how much we can lend. You will likely need Cash Down to cover the gap, or we must find a rare vehicle with huge Book Value.
Result: Limited Vehicle Options.

Zero / Positive Equity

Trade Value > Loan Balance (or No Trade)
(You have value or are starting fresh)

Good Credit (700+)
Strategy: Total Control

You can buy Older (Cheaper) cars with Long Terms, or Newer cars with Low Rates. You control the budget.
Result: Lowest Possible Payment.

New/Bad Credit (<650)
Strategy: The Stepping Stone

We must select a Newer Vehicle (2019+) so the bank has a warranty on the asset. We aim for approval first, Refinance later.
Result: Higher Payment (Short Term).
