Financing Your Vehicle

Your path is determined by your Equity Position & Credit Profile.

Equity Position

Negative Equity

Your loan amount is HIGHER than your vehicle's value.
Credit Profile

Good Credit (700+)

Strategy: Work with Banks to "Book Out"
  • Find a vehicle that banks value highly (e.g., specific Trucks/SUVs).
  • Banks are more flexible with strong credit, allowing debt consolidation.
  • Aim for a payment within your budget by adjusting terms.
Credit Profile

Bad / New Credit (<650)

Strategy: Down Payment is KEY
  • Banks have strict limits on how much they'll lend.
  • A significant down payment reduces the loan amount to meet bank rules.
  • Limited vehicle options until equity position is balanced.
Equity Position

Zero / Positive Equity

No trade-in, OR your trade-in is worth MORE than the loan.
Credit Profile

Good Credit (700+)

Strategy: You Have Control
  • Access to the lowest interest rates and longest terms.
  • Can choose almost any vehicle type that fits your budget.
  • Focus on achieving the lowest monthly payment or lowest total cost.
Credit Profile

Bad / New Credit (<650)

Strategy: Build Your Foundation
  • Focus on vehicles that "Book Well" with the bank (typically newer models).
  • Slightly higher interest rates are common, but approval is the goal.
  • Aim to make 12 on-time payments, then we can refinance for a better rate.
