Financing Logic Web

Follow the path to see how your Equity and Credit determine your vehicle options.

  • Financing A Vehicle
    • Situation
      Negative Equity
      You owe more on the loan than the car is currently worth.
      • Credit Profile
        Good Credit
        Score 700+
        The Strategy: Roll It Over
        • Vehicle: Must carry "High Book Value" (Truck/SUV).
        • Bank: Will approve the extra debt.
        • Result: Clean exit from old loan. Higher payment.
      • Credit Profile
        New / Bad Credit
        Score < 650
        The Strategy: Bank Cap
        • Restriction: Banks cap how much we can lend (LTV).
        • Solution: Need Cash Down OR a vehicle with massive rebates.
        • Result: Limited vehicle choices.
    • Situation
      Zero / Positive Equity
      You have no trade-in, OR your trade-in is worth more than the loan.
      • Credit Profile
        Good Credit
        Score 700+
        The Strategy: Total Control
        • Vehicle: Can buy Older (Cheaper) or Newer.
        • Term: Extended terms allowed (84mo+).
        • Result: Lowest possible monthly payment.
      • Credit Profile
        New / Bad Credit
        Score < 650
        The Strategy: Stepping Stone
        • Vehicle: Must be Newer (2018+) for warranty.
        • Term: Capped at 72 months.
        • Result: Higher payment, but high approval success.
