The Decision Web

Buying a vehicle isn't a straight line; it's a web of variables. Traditional dealerships force you into a box. We use a Decision Web to diagnose your Equity, Credit, and Goals to find the only path that makes mathematical sense.

START: YOUR SITUATION
Path 1: Trading A Vehicle
Managing your Equity Position
Positive Equity
Value > Loan Payoff
You have "Found Money." How do you want to use it?
Option A: The Down Payment
  • Lowers Payment: Reduces amount financed.
  • Tax Credit: You only pay tax on the difference (BC 12-15% Savings).
Option B: Cash Out
  • Liquidity: We write you a cheque for the equity.
  • Pros: Zero down, keep cash for emergencies.
  • Cons: Higher monthly payment.
Negative Equity
Loan Payoff > Value
The "Waterline" is underwater. We need a strategy.
Option A: The "Trapdoor"
  • Strategy: Buy an EV or High-Rebate vehicle.
  • Why: Use the $5k-$9k Rebate to "erase" your negative equity instantly.
  • Pros: Fresh start. Cons: Limited vehicle choice.
Option B: The Roll Over
  • Strategy: Consolidate debt into new loan.
  • Vehicle Requirement: Must buy "High Book Value" (Truck/SUV) to absorb the debt.
  • Result: Higher payment, but you exit the old loan.
