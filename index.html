  • Financing a Vehicle
    • Situation
      Negative Equity
      Loan Balance > Car Value
      • Good Credit (700+)
        Strategy: Consolidation
        Why do it?
        • Lower Interest Rate: Trade a high-rate loan for a lower prime rate.
        • Better Fit: Get a vehicle that actually suits your current needs.
        • Trade-off: Higher loan balance.
      • Building Credit (<650)
        Strategy: The "Book Out"
        Why do it?
        • Fresh Start: Exit a bad loan structure.
        • Reliability: Move into a vehicle with warranty.
        • Trade-off: Limited vehicle choice (Must have high book value).
    • Situation
      Positive / Zero Equity
      Car Value > Loan Balance
      • Good Credit (700+)
        Strategy: Total Control
        Why do it?
        • Lowest Payment: Use equity as down payment.
        • Flexibility: Buy Older (Cheaper) or Newer.
        • Tax Savings: Save 12-15% on trade value.
      • Building Credit (<650)
        Strategy: Foundation
        Why do it?
        • Approval: Equity helps secure the bank approval.
        • Refinance Plan: Re-rate in 12 months.
        • Trade-off: Restricted to newer models (2018+).
